About Shalabi Academy

Our site is basically designed to teach English to Thanawya Amma students. So, we provide students with:

  • Videos
  • Notes
  • Mind maps
  • Tests with model answers
  • Online testing
  • A dictionary for translation
  • Language skills courses: writing, translation .. etc
  • An easy way to communicate with Mr. Ali Shalabi and colleagues via Shalabi Academy Group

About Ali Shalabi

Mr. Ali Shalabi is a senior teacher of English at Abbaseya secondary school, Alexandria. He has twenty years' experience working in the governmental secondary schools and governmental secondary language schools. Years of experience vary between teaching in secondary schools and sharing in examinations work.
